# #68 EXP Checker

A smart, high-speed expiry date decipher tool designed for global citizens and expats. Part of the **Fridge Combo** series.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action
**EXP Checker** removes the confusion of mixed date formats (DMY vs. MDY vs. YMD) often found on imported food and products. Simply enter the numbers, and instantly see how many days are left until the potential expiry.

## Key Features
- **Smart Parsing**: Automatically handles dates with or without delimiters (e.g., `04/05/26`, `040526`, or `260405`).
- **Time-Distance Logic**: Displays results with "In X days" or "X days ago" labels, allowing you to intuitively judge the correct format based on your purchase timing.
- **Visual Urgency**: Uses a traffic-light color system:
  - 🟢 **Green**: Safe (7+ days left)
  - 🟠 **Orange**: Warning (Within 7 days)
  - 🔴 **Red**: Expired
- **Intelligent Sorting**: Prioritizes current/future dates. Dates from more than a year ago are automatically moved to the bottom and dimmed as "Archive" to reduce clutter.
- **Zero-Friction UI**: Large input box, instant results, and a 1-tap clear button.

## Design Philosophy
- **Minimalist Aesthetics**: Clean white background `#fff`, high-contrast text `#333`, and signature `15px` rounded corners.
- **Performance First**: Zero dependencies. Runs instantly on low-spec mobile devices and browsers.
- **Privacy Guaranteed**: No tracking, no ads, and no server-side processing. All date calculations happen locally on your device.

## How to Use
1. Locate the date code on your product packaging.
2. Type the numbers into the input field exactly as seen.
3. Compare the possible dates. The "time distance" (e.g., "In 30 days") will quickly tell you which format is the correct one for your item.
